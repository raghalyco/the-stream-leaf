Our Story

Built by family.
Run by family.

The Stream Leaf was built five years ago with a simple idea — to create a place near Mysore where families from Bangalore could come and truly rest. Not a managed hotel. Not a franchise. A personal space.

The name reflects the calm, green setting that inspired the property and the feeling of slowing down the moment you arrive.

After years of learning what our guests truly need, the founding family has personally taken back management of every aspect of The Stream Leaf — from the kitchen to the guest rooms. What you experience here is ours to own, and ours to improve.
